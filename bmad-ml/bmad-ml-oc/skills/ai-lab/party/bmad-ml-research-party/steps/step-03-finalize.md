# Step 03 - Finalize and Route

1. Synthesize findings into research-party-brief.md: topic, participants, format, central question, and summary of discussion.
2. Document areas of agreement with the supporting reasoning from each agent.
3. Document areas of tension or disagreement with each agent's position clearly stated.
4. List concrete recommendations that emerged from the discussion, ranked by confidence level (strong consensus, majority view, contested).
5. Identify follow-up questions that were raised but not resolved during the party.
6. List action items with suggested owners (which workflow or agent should pursue each item).
7. Note any surprises or insights that the agents surfaced that were not anticipated in the original question.
8. Present the research party brief for user review.

**Deliverables:** research-party-brief.md (with agent perspectives, agreement/tension map, synthesized recommendations, and follow-up questions).

**Recommended next workflow:** Depends on the topic -- typically `bmad-ml-experiment-design`, `bmad-ml-problem-formulation`, or `bmad-ml-feasibility-study` based on the party's recommendations.

> Quality check: Verify that (a) all agent perspectives are represented fairly, (b) agreements and tensions are clearly distinguished, (c) recommendations are actionable, and (d) the brief is concise enough to be useful as a reference.
