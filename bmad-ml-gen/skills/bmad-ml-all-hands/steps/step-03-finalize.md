# Step 03 - Finalize and Route

1. Produce all-hands-notes.md with sections: date, format, participants (labelled by division), agenda, decisions (labelled by owning division), action items, and parking lot.
2. List all decisions made during the meeting with: the decision statement, the rationale, any dissenting views, and the responsible owner.
3. List all action items with: description, owner, deadline, and acceptance criteria. Mark priority (high/medium/low).
4. List deferred topics (parking lot) with: brief description, reason for deferral, and proposed follow-up date or trigger.
5. Summarize the overall project status and trajectory: are things on track, at risk, or blocked?
6. Identify any workflow activations triggered by meeting decisions (e.g., "decided to run feasibility study" -> trigger bmad-ml-feasibility-study).
7. Update iteration history with the date, meeting format, key decisions, and action item count.
8. Present the completed meeting notes for user review.

**Deliverables:** all-hands-notes.md (with agenda outcomes, decisions with rationale, action items with owners and deadlines, and parking lot).

**Recommended next workflow:** Depends on meeting decisions -- action items specify the workflows to activate next.

> Quality check: Verify that (a) every agenda item has an outcome documented, (b) all action items have owners and deadlines, (c) decisions include rationale, and (d) parking lot items have follow-up plans.
